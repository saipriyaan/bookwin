How It Works

Step 1: Submit Your Photo

We require a high-definition image where the face is facing straight, and no objects are blocking the view. This helps us ensure precision in our portrait transformation.

Step 2: Feedback and Approval

After submission, we provide feedback on your image. If your submission doesn't meet our guidelines, we will cancel the order. Otherwise, we proceed with creating a sample for your approval.

Step 3: Sample & Payment

We send a sample preview for you to review. Payment is only required after you approve the sample. There are no refunds as payment is done post-approval.

Step 4: Final Delivery

Once the payment is complete, the final artwork is sent to you via email or a download link in our company chat. The entire process takes about 7 working days.
